About Cav's AlgoRhythms

Cav's AlgoRhythms is an online song store that offers a wide selection of classic and contemporary songs and albums. This website has been created as an SEO contest project for the CSCE 470 class at Texas A&M University, taught by the one and only Professor James Caverlee, the founder and owner of Cav's AlgoRhythms.

What is SEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is The process of improving a website to increase its visibility on search engines like Google and Bing. It uses a variety of strategies, including link development, content production, on-page optimization, and keyword research. SEO's primary goal is to increase organic traffic to a website and to have a better webpage ranking on search engines for targeted queries.
